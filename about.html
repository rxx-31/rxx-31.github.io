about

A cozy corner for handcrafted coffee, warm conversations, and delicious bites.

background

Founded in 2022, upper east side began as a simple idea: create a space where quality coffee meets genuine community. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into the welcoming coffee shop you know today.

Our journey began when a group of friends with a shared love for exceptional coffee decided to bring something special to the neighborhood. We believed that coffee should be more than just a morning ritual—it should be an experience that brings people together.

Today, upper east side stands as a testament to that vision. We've created a space where friends meet, ideas flow, and every cup tells a story.

our coffee

We source only the finest beans from ethical suppliers around the world. Each batch is carefully selected and roasted to perfection to bring out unique flavor profiles that delight the senses.

our space

Designed with comfort in mind, our café offers a warm atmosphere where you can work, chat, or simply unwind. Local artwork adorns our walls, and each corner invites you to stay a little longer.

our community

More than just serving coffee, we're committed to building relationships. We regularly host events, workshops, and gatherings that bring people together and strengthen our community ties.

our journey
